Plumbing Business: City-Wide Map Pack Campaign

A plumbing business operating across Sydney suburbs had zero visibility in the Google Maps results when we started. The geo-grid showed 20+ positions (worst possible) across the entire target area. After a full local SEO campaign covering GBP optimization, citation building, review velocity, and service area content, the business broke into top-3 positions across key suburbs.

What drove the improvement

GBP Optimization

Full audit and rebuild of the Google Business Profile including primary and secondary categories, service list, photos, attributes, and description optimization.

Review Velocity

Implemented a review generation system that increased review count and recency, both key local ranking signals for Google Maps.

Citation Building

Cleaned up and built consistent NAP citations across major Australian business directories and industry-specific platforms.

Location Content

Created suburb-specific service pages targeting high-intent searches across the client's key service areas in Sydney.
